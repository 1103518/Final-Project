How to play?

The Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game is a turn-based card game where you 《Duel》 against opponents and their 《Deck》 of cards!

The objective of the game is to 《Summon》 monster cards from your 《Hand》 to attack, and reduce your opponent's 《LP》 to 0!
